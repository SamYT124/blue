BLUE

What is blue?

Blue is the color between green and violet. It is one of the primary colors of light as well as pigment. Also, to be "blue" means to be sad or depressed. Blue is the color of this article.

Why is blue important?

The color blue signifies trust, stability, calmness, and wisdom across many cultures. It is known to lower blood pressure and heart rate. Without blue, colors like purple or violet will not exist.
